Hi! I'm Samaira.

I'm a marketing major at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business, writing this from a small desk that always has too much paint on it. I care about consumer behavior, brand strategy, and the quiet mechanics that make a thing feel chosen — not sold.

My background in content creation pulled me into the strategic side of marketing: how brands position themselves, how audiences decide, how loyalty is actually built across years and not campaigns. Through academic work, Designers & Business, and volunteer experience, I try to keep equal parts creativity and analytical thinking on the page.

Outside of school I paint, sew, edit short videos, and recently started spending time with CNC tools — which I love for the same reason I love a good thriller: precision and patience with the payoff at the end.

I'm currently looking for opportunities in brand strategy, digital marketing, and consumer insights — and always for new things to read.
